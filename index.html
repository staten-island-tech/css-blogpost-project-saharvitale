My Favorite Desserts

Cannolis

Cannolis are Italian pastries that originated in Sicily. Most cannoli are made up of tube-shaped shells of fried pastry dough filled with a sweet cream.

cannoli recipe

Mochi

Mochi is a Japanese rice cake made of mochigome and other ingredients such as water, sugar, and cornstarch. It has a soft, chewy texture and is often topped or flavored with matcha or red bean powder.

mochi recipes

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a thick dessert made up of a mixture of a soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar, with a crust as the bottom layer. It can be plated with many different toppings such as fruits or chocolate.

cheesecake recipe

Rice Pudding

My personal favorite, rice pudding is a dish made from rice mixed with water or milk and other ingredients such as cinnamon, vanilla and raisins.
